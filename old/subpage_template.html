Regler

Eligibility

Each team needs to consist of 5 eligible (read below) players and one of which to be a team leader. Predetermined teams shall be required by the each players confirmation and must provide .DLL account with the addition of the team name.
Non- predetermined team/players who doesn’t consist of a full team will be given the chance of eligibility by matching with other people in advanced of the event. Although more information will be given at a later point.

Format

The tournament will include X of teams.
Single elimination format is used by the entirety of the event.

Administrators

Players should always address the administrators and each other in a polite and respectfulway.
An administrator has the final say in all matters. When the administrator says the word is final, the word is final and cannot be overruled.
Under some circumstances administrators reserve the right to change the rules.

General Rules

Server

All games must be played on the Eu West. Player account must therefore be on Eu west.

Game preparations

Team captains should be contactable at all time during the tournament, in the case of previously unknown people put together in one team, we will determine the procedures at a later date for a easier way of one team connecting before the event.
The players should arrive in in­game chat (.dll) at least 15 minutes before their match starts.
By this time the players should be set up and ready to play.
For finals, the players should be ready to set up (at the latest) 30min before the match starts. Should a player be late (more than 20 minutes) for the start of a tournament match, the player will forfeit the first game of the match.
Should the player be more than 30 minutes late for the start of a tournament match, the player will forfeit the match.
The players are allowed a 5 minute break between the games in a tournamentmatch.(10min for finals).
Administrators reserve the right to change these timings if circumstances require it.

Disconnects and computer failure

If one or more players disconnects from the game due to technical reasons and the game is heavily favoured to the extent that the game would definitely have been won by one team an administrator may rule the game in favors of said team. Should the game be too close to call, it will be replayed.
Administrators reserve the right to rule against previous rules. The restart of game will be in the determination of extended rules

Complaints

If there a complaint occurring during the game contact the judges, that will look into the matter. With the right to stop the game if an infraction against the rules has occurred.
If a player wants to make a complaint about a game result, the player has to tell the administrator assigned to the match immediately following the game.
If a player wants to object a game result ruled by an administrator, this has to be brought forth by the team captain.

Punishments

Should a player or team purposely attempt to sabotage the games, another player or an administrator in any way, the player will be disqualified.
Bad behaviour towards other players, administrators or the audience may result in a disqualification.

Chatting

  • No chat is ever permitted from a player to an outside party during a tournament match, or from an outside party to the player, unless the outside party is an administrator.

Game settings

  • No other IM applications, or streams are allowed to be running in the background.
  • All players are required to join a special Tournament channel given at the location. (.dll)

Bugs and fair play

  • No script or highlighting programs is allowed to be used, or the team is disqualified.
  • No abuse of bugs in the game is allowed in any tournament match.
  • If a player is clearly allowing the opposite player to win, said player may be warned or disqualified pending how clearly the match was lost on purpose.

Observers and streaming

  • No observers are allowed in any tournament games, except for assigned streamers and administrators.
  • Players may not watch or listen to the stream of the tournament while playing.

Picking

  • The normal procedure of picks and bans will go along as normal, with the addition of reservation of administrators to pre-game ban characters seemed unfit for a fair game. These will be reserved to use in the outmost need.
Rule changes in case of Riot sponsored prized event

We the administrators will reserve the rule to change any and all rules pre-event, since the matter of a riot sponsored event is still being determined.
